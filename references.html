How answer explanations are structured

A page number by itself is not enough for revision. Each revealed answer now contains:

  1. Your result — the selected answer, correct answer and marks awarded.
  2. Decision path — the tested topic, decisive scenario fact and rule that resolves the question.
  3. Guide meaning in plain English — an original paraphrase of the relevant PRINCE2 concept.
  4. Option-by-option analysis — why each choice is correct or incorrect, including the option selected by the learner.
  5. Official guide location — chapter, section, printed page and PDF-viewer page where available.
  6. Alternative explanations — video, PeopleCert, professional-body and training-provider articles.

The guide summaries are paraphrased rather than copied as long extracts. This keeps the explanations readable and avoids republishing the official manual while still showing where the exact wording can be verified.
